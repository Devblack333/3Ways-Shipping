About ThreeWays Shipping

ThreeWays is a full-service logistics provider combining global carrier agreements, proprietary routing algorithms, and hands-on customs expertise to make international shipping predictable and simple.

We design transit plans tailored to your priorities — speed, cost, or reliability — and provide a single point of contact for complex shipments, including temperature-controlled cargo and oversized freight.

Our Services

Air Freight

Door-to-door and airport-to-airport solutions for urgent and time-sensitive cargo. We work with major airlines and consolidators, offer priority handling, customs pre-clearance, and cargo insurance options to protect high-value goods during transit.

Sea Freight

Comprehensive ocean freight services including FCL (full container load), LCL (less-than-container), door pickups, and port-to-door delivery. We negotiate competitive carrier rates, provide container tracking, and handle marine insurance and cargo consolidation.

Ground & Road Transport

Regional distribution, palletized freight and last-mile delivery with GPS-tracked vehicles and scheduled routes. Our ground network supports multi-modal handoffs to bridge sea/air shipments to final destinations efficiently.

Warehousing & Fulfillment

Secure warehousing, inventory management, kitting, and order fulfillment services. We support short-term storage, pick-and-pack workflows, and EDI/API integrations for automated fulfillment pipelines.
